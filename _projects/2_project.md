Social media platforms publish transparency reports to communicate how they handle harmful content — but how accurate and complete are these reports? This project conducts a mixed-methods analysis of transparency reports across major platforms, surfacing systematic gaps between reported policies and observable outcomes.

Published as a Late-Breaking Work at **CHI 2025** {% cite chang2025opaque %}.

**Methods:** Qualitative content analysis, policy document analysis.

**Collaborators:** Tyler Chang, Sharon Bassan, Afsaneh Razi (DONUTS Lab, Drexel University)
