This project examines the comment sections of TikTok videos tagged with **#Harassment**, asking how viewers respond to content depicting harassment. Using topic modeling and content analysis on a large corpus of comments, we characterized the types of support, dismissal, and engagement that emerge in these spaces.

Published as a Late-Breaking Work at **CHI 2024** {% cite armin2024support %}.

**Methods:** Novel social media data collection, topic modeling, LIWC psycholinguistic analysis.

**Collaborators:** Atieh Armin, Jordyn Young, Afsaneh Razi (DONUTS Lab, Drexel University)
